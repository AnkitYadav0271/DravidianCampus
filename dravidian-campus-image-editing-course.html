Photoshop & Animation

Learn how to edit photos and make moving animations from start to finish. Master photo editing, picture design, cartoon motion, and make real projects.

About the Course

Photo editing and animation are great skills to learn today. This course teaches you how to change pictures, design graphics, and make objects move using simple tools.

You will start from the basics and move to making your own animations. You will get real practice by working on fun projects and daily exercises.

What You Will Learn

  • check_circle Photoshop Tools and How to Use Them
  • check_circle Photo Editing and Changing Backgrounds
  • check_circle Making Logos and Banner Designs
  • check_circle Color Corrections and Retouching
  • check_circle Wedding Album Editing
  • check_circle Basics of Animation and Moving Objects
  • check_circle Motion Graphics
  • check_circle Video Editing (Intro/Outro/Reels)

Before You Start

  • Basic computer knowledge
  • No past design or drawing skills needed
  • Ready to practice every day

Who Can Join?

  • School Students
  • College Students
  • Content Creators
  • Anyone who wants to learn design

By the end of this course, you will be able to edit photos and create your own animations easily.
