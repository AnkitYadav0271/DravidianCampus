Tally Prime Course

Master the world's most popular business accounting software. Learn inventory management, GST compliance, taxation, and financial reporting to fast-track your career in corporate accounting.

About the Course

Efficient accounting is the backbone of every growing business. This hands-on Tally Prime course is designed to take you from a foundational understanding of accounting principles to advanced business data management.

Through real-world case studies and regular practical lab sessions, you will confidently learn how to set up company books, manage accounts receivable/payable, handle payroll systems, and generate error-free financial returns.

What You Will Learn

  • check_circle Company Creation and Ledger Management
  • check_circle Advanced Inventory and Stock Tracking
  • check_circle GST Invoicing, E-Way Bills, and Tax Filings
  • check_circle Bank Reconciliation Statements (BRS)
  • check_circle Corporate Payroll and Employee Salary Management
  • check_circle Profit & Loss Statements and Balance Sheets
  • check_circle Real-time Industry Case Drills
  • check_circle Keyboard Shortcuts for High-Speed Accounting

Before You Start

  • Basic understanding of computer operations
  • Familiarity with foundational commerce concepts is helpful but not mandatory
  • Commitment to complete assignments and lab projects regularly

Who Can Join?

  • Commerce Graduates & MBA Students
  • Aspiring Accountants & Bookkeepers
  • Business Owners & Entrepreneurs
  • Anyone wanting to secure high-paying office jobs

By the end of this course, you will be handling complex business account portfolios smoothly and professionally.
